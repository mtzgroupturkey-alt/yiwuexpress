## Important Notes:

1. **Top Bar Location:**
   - At the VERY TOP of the page
   - Dark blue background
   - BEFORE the logo and navigation

2. **Visibility:**
   - Shows on desktop (width > 768px)
   - Hides on mobile/tablet
   - Slides up when you scroll down
   - Reappears when you scroll to top

3. **Animation:**
   - Text types character by character
   - Cursor "|" blinks while waiting
   - Pauses 2.6 seconds when complete
   - Deletes and shows next message
   - Loops forever

## What Each Element Does:

```
✦           → Sparkle icon (decorative, gold color)
Text        → Types character by character
|           → Cursor (blinks with GSAP animation)
Background  → Dark blue (#1a3a5c)
Text Color  → White with 60% opacity
```
