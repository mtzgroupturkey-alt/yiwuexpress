# ⚡ DUPLICATE FEATURE - QUICK GUIDE

**Save 5+ minutes per slide with one-click duplication!**

---

## 🎯 WHAT IS IT?

A **Copy button** (📋) that duplicates any hero slide instantly with all its content and settings.

---

## 🚀 HOW TO USE (3 Steps)

### **Step 1:** Go to Admin
```
http://localhost:3001/admin/settings/hero-slider
```

### **Step 2:** Click Copy Button
Find the slide you want → Click the **📋** button

### **Step 3:** Confirm & Edit
- Click **OK** in confirmation dialog
- Wait for success toast
- Edit the new slide (inactive by default)
- Remove " (Copy)" from title
- Activate when ready!

---

## ✨ WHAT GETS COPIED

**Everything!**
- ✅ All images
- ✅ Title, subtitle, description
- ✅ Badges and colors
- ✅ CTA buttons
- ✅ Duration
- ✅ Motion type
- ✅ All settings

**New slide is:**
- 📍 At the end of the list
- 🔴 Inactive (won't show publicly)
- 📝 Title has " (Copy)" appended
