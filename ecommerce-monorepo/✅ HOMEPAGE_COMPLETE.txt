🎯 HOW TO MANAGE PRODUCTS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

ADD PRODUCTS TO FEATURED/NEW ARRIVALS:
  1. Go to: http://localhost:3000/admin/products
  2. Find product in table
  3. Toggle "Featured" switch ON → Product appears in Featured
  4. Toggle "New Arrival" switch ON → Product appears in New Arrivals
  5. Refresh homepage → Changes are instant!

REORDER PRODUCTS:
  1. Go to: http://localhost:3000/admin/settings/featured-products
  2. Drag products by grip handle (⋮⋮) to reorder
  3. Order saves automatically
  4. Refresh homepage → New order displays

REMOVE PRODUCTS:
  1. Go to: http://localhost:3000/admin/products
  2. Toggle "Featured" or "New Arrival" switch OFF
  3. Refresh homepage → Product removed from section
